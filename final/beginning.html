The Beginning

How does a pandemic start? It takes one person.

A pandemic occurs when a disease spreads over a country, and ultimately, the world. Near the end of 2019, Wuhan, China began treating the first patients infected with the Coronavirus. 19 days after the first reported death in China, the virus had spread to the United States, according to The New York Times. In the span of three months the virus led to social distancing, business and school closures, and fear. The Coronavirus has altered everyone's daily lives, causing indiviudals to be more consciously aware of their health and interactions with their communities. But what about the subconscious effects? How has the virus impacted the mental health of individuals through different age groups? Let's find out.
